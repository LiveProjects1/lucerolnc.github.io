ABOUT

Bringing Your Outdoor Dreams to Life: Learn More About Our Passion for Landscaping!

Crafting Nature's Masterpieces for Your Outdoor Oasis.

Welcome to Lucero Landscaping & Construction, your premier destination for exceptional landscaping and construction services in Montville, New Jersey, and the surrounding areas. With over 20 years of industry experience, we pride ourselves on delivering personalized solutions tailored to our clients' unique needs and visions. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to transforming your outdoor spaces into beautiful, functional, and sustainable environments. From creative landscape design and installation to high-quality construction projects, we cover all aspects of your property improvement needs.

Services

We provide a complete list of landscape & hardscape needs.

Planting

Partnering with you, we will carefully select an array of trees, shrubs, annuals, and perennials that perfectly complement your yard, then professionally plant them to ensure their flourishing growth.

Falls / springs clean-ups

Let us handle the task of removing leaves, branches, and other debris from your property on a regular basis, keeping your outdoor space clean and tidy without any hassle on your part.

Design

With our expertise and your imagination, we can transform your yard into a stunning masterpiece, no matter how big or small your initial plans may be.

Trimming /pruning

Transform your landscaping with a deep summer pruning that promotes healthy plant and tree growth while keeping your yard neatly shaped year-round

Thatching & Aeration

Achieve a healthy, lush lawn with our expert dethatching in spring and core aeration in fall, ensuring your yard looks vibrant all year.

Drainage & Re-grading

Let us transform your yard with a proper drainage system that maximizes its beauty and functionality, solving any water problems for good.

Mulching

Enhance your plant beds with our fresh Cedar, Hemlock, and Bark mulch options in blonde, brown, and red, providing a beautiful complement to your yard.

Weekly Grass Cutting

Keep your yard in perfect shape with our weekly or bi-weekly services, including thorough edging, weed removal, and grass clipping disposal.

Seed & sod installation

Whether you need general over-seeding, brand new seeded lawn, or complete weed-free sod installation, we have you covered.

Snow removal

For both residential and commercial properties, we offer 24-hour snow removal services to meet all of your winter weather needs.

Fence Installation

Upgrade your yard with beautiful, functional fencing options like vinyl, wooden, and chain link to achieve the look and privacy you desire

Retaining Walls

Add dimension and stability to your yard with our expertly designed retaining walls, from simple garden blocks to large-scale solutions tailored to your needs.

Cultured stone

Transform the exterior of your home with the addition of cultured stone, adding a beautiful and durable finish to your house foundation, stairs, fireplace, and more.

Patios, walkways, Driveways

Elevate your hardscape design by selecting from our extensive selection of brick paver systems, available in a diverse range of colors, textures, and patterns to make your outdoor space truly unique.

Expert Landscaping: Four Simple Steps

Experience Stress-Free Landscaping in Just Four Steps: Our Tried-and-True Method for Making Your Outdoor Space Beautiful and Functional

  • STEP 1

    Contact Us

    The first step is to reach out to us either by phone or by filling out our online contact form. We'll ask you for some basic information about your landscaping needs, including your location, the type of services you're interested in, and your budget.

  • STEP 2

    Free Consultation

    After we receive your request, we'll schedule a free consultation with you to discuss your landscaping needs in more detail. During this consultation, we'll assess your property and provide you with expert advice on how to enhance your outdoor space.

  • STEP 3

    Proposal

    Once we have a clear understanding of your landscaping needs and preferences, we'll develop a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work, materials, and estimated cost. We'll work with you to make any necessary revisions to ensure that the proposal meets your expectations.

  • STEP 4

    Installation

    After you've approved the proposal, we'll schedule a date to begin the installation process. Our team of skilled landscapers will arrive on-site and transform your outdoor space according to the plan we developed together. We'll work efficiently to ensure that the project is completed on-time and within budget
